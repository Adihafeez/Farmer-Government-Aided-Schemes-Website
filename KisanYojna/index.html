About Project

Welcome to KisanYojna App, a pioneering National Platform developed as part of our college project for the subject of Web-Based Programming. Implemented using PHP, our platform serves as a comprehensive solution, offering a single point of access for citizens to explore and discover various Government schemes related to agriculture.

KisanYojna App empowers users to effortlessly navigate through a myriad of scheme information, tailored to their specific eligibility criteria.

With KisanYojna App, users can easily identify and apply for the schemes that best suit their agricultural needs. Furthermore, our platform provides step-by-step guidance on the application process.
